We've got your petz covered!

KamiPetz is the ultimate digital platform for professional and specialized pet care, featuring expert veterinarians, grooming, boarding, marketplace and more in one.
Digitally powered - locally present.

Vision:

To improve the overall animal health in Southeast Asia starting with Indonesia by making pet care easily accessible through digital solutions, and by creating a strong community and support to member veterinarians, animal hospitals, clinics, groomers, pet shops and the like.

Mission:

To improve the accessibility and quality of pet care today and prepare for the needs of tomorrow creating a 360 platform of deliverables for pet owners.
